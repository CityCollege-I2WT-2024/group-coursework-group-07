PERFORMING AND
VISUAL ARTS

FIND YOUR OWN CINEMATIC EXPERIENCE

Olympion Cinema

Located in Aristotelous Square, Olympion Cinema is an iconic venue that hosts the Thessaloniki International Film Festival and offers a range of classic and contemporary films.

Cineplexx Thessaloniki

A modern multiplex cinema offering the latest blockbusters, 3D screenings, and comfortable seating, located in the Mediterranean Cosmos shopping mall.

Village Cinemas

Known for its state-of-the-art technology and immersive experience, Village Cinemas offers a wide selection of international and Greek films.

Apollon Cinema

A historic cinema in the heart of Thessaloniki, Apollon Cinema offers a mix of mainstream and arthouse films in a nostalgic setting.

EXPERIENCE THE MAGIC OF LIVE PERFORMANCES

National Theatre of Northern Greece

One of the largest and most prestigious theaters in Greece, offering a wide range of performances including classical plays, contemporary dramas, and musicals.

Royal Theatre

A historic venue known for its stunning architecture and diverse programming, featuring everything from Greek tragedies to modern performances.

Avlaia Theatre

A smaller, intimate theater that focuses on experimental and avant-garde productions, providing a platform for emerging artists.

Theatre of the Earth

An open-air theater offering a unique experience with performances ranging from classical Greek drama to contemporary plays, all set against the backdrop of nature.
