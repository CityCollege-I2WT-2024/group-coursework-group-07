Events and Festivals

don't miss out in Thessaloniki

Thessaloniki hosts a variety of vibrant annual events that celebrate its rich culture and history. Enjoy lively street fairs, food festivals, and traditional celebrations that showcase the city's dynamic spirit and offer unique experiences for everyone.

From the electrifying Reworks Festival for electronic music fans to the culturally rich Street Mode Festival, thereâ€™s something for everyone. These festivals offer unforgettable experiences that highlight the vibrant spirit of the city.

ANNUAL EVENTS

Thessaloniki International Fair

The Thessaloniki International Fair (TIF) is one of the largest and most significant trade fairs in Greece. Held every September, TIF brings together exhibitors from a wide range of industries, including technology, agriculture, fashion, and food. The fair provides a platform for businesses to showcase their products and services, network with potential partners, and explore new market opportunities. In addition to the trade exhibitions, TIF features cultural events, concerts, and entertainment activities, making it a vibrant and dynamic event that attracts thousands of visitors each year.

Date: September

Thessaloniki Book Fair

The Thessaloniki Book Fair is a prominent literary event that takes place every May. The fair gathers publishers, authors, translators, and book lovers from Greece and beyond. It features book presentations, readings, panel discussions, and workshops. The Thessaloniki Book Fair aims to promote literature and reading culture, providing a platform for literary exchange and dialogue. The fair also includes special sections for children's books, educational materials, and digital publishing, catering to a wide range of interests and audiences.

Date: May

ANNUAL FESTIVALS

Thessaloniki International Film Festival

The Thessaloniki International Film Festival (TIFF) is one of the oldest and most prestigious film festivals in Southeast Europe. Each November, the festival attracts filmmakers, actors, critics, and cinema enthusiasts from around the world. TIFF showcases a diverse range of films, including feature films, documentaries, and short films. It is renowned for its international competition section, where emerging filmmakers compete for the coveted Golden Alexander Award. The festival also features masterclasses, panel discussions, and workshops, providing a comprehensive cinematic experience.

Date: November

Reworks Festival

Reworks Festival is an acclaimed electronic music festival held in Thessaloniki every September. The festival brings together top international and local DJs, producers, and electronic music artists for a weekend of electrifying performances. Reworks is known for its innovative approach to music and its ability to blend different genres and styles. In addition to music performances, the festival features art installations, visual performances, and workshops, creating a multi-sensory experience for attendees.

Date: September

Street Mode Festival

A dynamic celebration of street culture, featuring live music, street art, dance competitions, and extreme sports. Street Mode Festival transforms Thessaloniki into a hub of creativity with graffiti, BMX, skateboarding, and breakdancing performances. Interactive workshops and vibrant energy make this festival a must-attend for all ages.

Date: September

Thessaloniki Food Festival

The Thessaloniki Food Festival is an annual event that celebrates the city's rich culinary heritage. Held every November, the festival features a variety of food-related activities, including cooking demonstrations, food tastings, and culinary workshops. Local and international chefs showcase their skills, and visitors can sample a wide array of dishes from traditional Greek cuisine to modern culinary creations. The Thessaloniki Food Festival is a must-visit event for food enthusiasts.

Date: November
