Experience Thessaloniki by Day

Thessaloniki by day is a vibrant blend of history, culture, and modern flair. Begin your morning with a leisurely coffee in one of the city’s countless kafeneia (cafés). Thessaloniki is famous for having the most cafés per capita in Europe, making coffee an integral part of daily life. Whether you prefer a Greek freddo espresso or a cozy spot with artisanal brews, districts like Aristotelous Square, Ladadika, and the charming streets of Ano Poli offer atmospheric café experiences perfect for soaking in the city vibes

"A cup of coffee shared with a friend is happiness tasted and time well spent."

Did You Know?

Thessaloniki has a strong coffee culture, with cafes offering everything from Greek coffee to espresso, making them perfect for socializing or working.

Areas like Ladadika and Aristotelous Square are full of trendy cafes, many with a bohemian or vintage vibe.

La Doze Bar offers a great view of the sea and the White Tower, making it a perfect spot for coffee and people-watching.
