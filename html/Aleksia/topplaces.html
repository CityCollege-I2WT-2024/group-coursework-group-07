La Tagliatella

Cuisine Type: Italian

Address: 26 Vilara Street, Thessaloniki 54625, Greece

Short Description: A cozy and stylish Italian restaurant known for its wide selection of pasta and traditional Italian dishes. The ambiance is warm, making it a great spot for a casual yet quality meal.

Price Range: €€ (Average meal for two: €20-€30)

Recommended Dishes:

  • Tagliatelle al Tartufo (Truffle Pasta)
  • Pizza Quattro Formaggi
  • Tiramisu

Modiano Market (Food Hall)

Cuisine Type: Greek, Mediterranean

Address: Modiano Market, Thessaloniki 54625, Greece

Short Description: This historic indoor market offers an authentic Greek food experience with a variety of local vendors serving fresh Greek delicacies, including meats, cheeses, olives, and traditional snacks.

Price Range: € (Meals and snacks range from €5-€15)

Recommended Dishes:

  • Bougatsa (sweet or savory pastry)
  • Souvlaki
  • Greek olives and cheese varieties

Sense Restaurant

Cuisine Type: Fine Dining, Modern Greek

Address: 6 Vilara Street, Thessaloniki 54625, Greece

Short Description: A Michelin-star restaurant offering an innovative twist on Greek cuisine, blending local ingredients with contemporary techniques. It’s perfect for special occasions.

Price Range: €€€€ (Fine dining, average meal for two: €80-€120)

Recommended Dishes:

  • Lamb with smoked eggplant
  • Octopus with local herbs
  • Greek dessert with honey and yogurt

Ouzeri Aristotelous

Cuisine Type: Greek, Seafood

Address: Aristotelous Square, Thessaloniki 54625, Greece

Short Description: A popular spot for fresh seafood and traditional Greek ouzo, located near the famous Aristotelous Square. It’s a great place to enjoy a meal while people-watching in the heart of the city.

Price Range: €€ (Average meal for two: €25-€35)

Recommended Dishes:

  • Grilled sardines
  • Ouzo with meze platters
  • Fresh octopus

Family-owned Traditional Greek Restaurant

Cuisine Type: Greek, Traditional

Address: 13 Valaoritou Street, Thessaloniki 54625, Greece

Short Description: A cozy, family-owned restaurant known for serving delicious traditional Greek dishes in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. It’s particularly famous for its hearty meat dishes and local specialties.

Price Range: €€ (Average meal for two: €20-€30)

Recommended Dishes:

  • Moussaka
  • Lamb with herbs
  • Greek salad
